What are Enterprise Networking Products?

Enterprise Networking Products are the networking products built for business. They are all certified and passed all the required tests (usually much more extreme tests than home consumming networking products) before getting out to the market.

Enterprise Networking products are always built for Security, Performance, 24/7 Workload. A standard Enterprise Networking system usually has a Security Gateway, an Ethernet Switch, and serveral Access Points.

For these reasons, the Enterprise Networking products are very expensive. For example, a standard networking system provided by Cisco usually costs more than $1,000 USD.

However, there is a company selling the same Enterprise Networking products with much afforadble price than Cisco, which is UBNT (Ubiquiti Networks). Their standard networking system usually cost around $600 USD.
